Welcome

Hi, My name's Annabelle and I am a student game developer studying at Abertay University in Dundee.

This website contains projects I have worked on, most are free to download and use/play.

Most projects developed with C++, C# (Unity), Java, Javascript (inc. NodeJS)

Portfolio

Floated

A Game developed for the 2017 Global Game Jam

Use waves to move a colour co-ordinated bottle to the correct island, developed by a 4 person team.

Code and game functionality developed by myself.

Shade

Abertay University Dare Academy Entry.

Shade is a third person MMO shooter developed by a 5 person team.

Networking and Server-side functionality developed by myself.

Swemark and Denden's Great Eggventure

Systematically Transmitted Infection

Game developed by myself.

Mod developed by myself.
