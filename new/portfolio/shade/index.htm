Shade

Abertay University Dare Academy Entry.

Shade was developed by a 5 person team. Networking and Server-side functionality developed by myself in Unity, NodeJS using Docker and Redis services.

Shade was shown at EGX 2017 in the rezzed zone.

Shade is an online, multiplayer, isometric shooter. Fight your way to the top of the leaderboard in this fast paced, arcade style game. Earn your place in the hall of fame by making it onto the all time high-score boards.

Target other players for higher scores, or hide in the shadows to try and live longer!

Power ups litter the map which can give you an advantage over another players, as well as help improving your score. If you have a power up or you kill someone with a power up you will gain extra points.

Every kill will increase your score multiplier! However, if you don't keep up the fight, you will start to lose any gained multiplier quickly.

Shade is currently not available due to lack of server hosting.
